# Apprentice 2.2.2c

- Gives every race a deliberate facial identity instead of only changing its
  body scale and skin color.
- Assigns race-specific facial expression, eye color, hairstyle and facial
  hair where appropriate.
- Combines these selections with the correctly anchored racial ear, nose,
  horn and tusk shapes introduced by the race-body system.
- Saves the player's original face and eye selections and restores them when
  switching back to Human.
