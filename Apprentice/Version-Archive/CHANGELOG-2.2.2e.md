# Apprentice 2.2.2e

- Keeps the approved Dwarf, Elf, Gnome and Halfling faces unchanged.
- Replaces Tiefling's manually positioned horns with native head-anchored horn geometry.
- Removes Goliath's oversized facial blocks and lets its scale, complexion and proportions define the race.
- Replaces Orc's bar-like muzzle with pointed ears and small lower tusks.
- Replaces Dragonborn's cube head and bar snout with a restrained native-compatible projecting nose profile.
- Adds a warm bone texture for horns and tusks.
