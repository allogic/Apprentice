# Apprentice 2.1.14h — Vertically Centered Professions

## Interface

- Moved the shared profession-name baseline down by four design units.
- Profession names such as **Warrior**, **Ranger**, **Spearman**, and
  **Shield** are now centered both horizontally and vertically in their rows.
- Profession icons, level counters, row bounds, and hit regions are unchanged.
- All 2.1.14g skill-tree label and dialog-frame refinements are retained.

## Compatibility

- Existing saves remain compatible.
- Tree IDs, node IDs, hidden-class IDs, and tab IDs are unchanged.
