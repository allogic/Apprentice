# Apprentice 2.2.2f

- Replaces the Orc moustache-tusk asset with two-piece lower-jaw tusks that
  rise vertically instead of sweeping sideways.
- Rebuilds the Dragonborn face around a projecting upper muzzle, separate
  lower jaw, broad nose cap, reptilian brow and cheek plates, forehead ridge,
  chin and paired backswept horns.
- Gives Goliaths a restrained but clearly non-human stone-giant face with a
  heavy divided brow, broad nose, angular cheekbones, square jaw and small
  forehead lithoderms.
- Keeps the approved Dwarf, Elf, Gnome, Halfling and Tiefling shapes unchanged.
- Keeps all additions attached to the native Head bone so normal animations,
  armor, clothing and first-/third-person rendering remain on the standard
  player skeleton.
