# Apprentice 2.2.2d

- Rebuilds every race face as correctly head-anchored geometry.
- Dragonborn now have a projecting two-part snout, reptilian brow, nostrils
  and paired horns.
- Orcs now have a broad muzzle and jaw, heavy brow, tusks and pointed ears.
- Tieflings now have swept two-part horns, pointed ears, angular brows and
  pronounced cheek structure.
- Elves, dwarves, gnomes, halflings and goliaths receive distinct ear, nose,
  brow, cheek and jaw silhouettes appropriate to their proportions.
- Preserves the race-specific hairstyles, expressions, eye colors and Human
  restoration behavior introduced by the racial-face system.
