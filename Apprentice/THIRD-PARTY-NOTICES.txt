OverhaulLib Legacy
==================

Apprentice's War Scythe animation pipeline contains adapted portions of
OverhaulLib Legacy at commit:

    9c49c1865acaf014012884d3c4a93c8eabf08bdb

Upstream project authors: Maltiez and Alodh
Upstream license: MIT

The adapted source is contained in:

    src/animation/reference/Animation.cs
    src/animation/reference/Animator.cs
    src/animation/reference/Composer.cs
    src/animation/reference/EasingFunctions.cs
    src/animation/reference/Frames.cs
    src/animation/reference/AnimationEditorHistory.cs
    src/animation/reference/AnimationSinks.cs
    src/animation/reference/ReferenceAnimationEditing.cs

ApprenticeAnimationHook.cs and ApprenticeAnimationSystem.cs adapt the same
OverhaulLib Legacy matrix-insertion, animator-mapping, render-frame, and
separate first/third-person ownership contracts for Apprentice's single War
Scythe animation.
